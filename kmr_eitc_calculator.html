Methodology Notes: Assumes the standard deduction in all cases. The Child Tax Credit (CTC) is applied for eligible children (income above $2,500; MAGI below phase-out thresholds). The EITC for childless workers is subject to additional age restrictions (ages 25–64) not captured here. MAGI is treated as earned income for credit phase-in calculations. All figures reflect 2026 federal parameters only; state and local taxes are excluded. FPL percentages reflect the 2026 HHS Poverty Guidelines for the 48 contiguous states (effective January 13, 2026); household size equals the number of adults (1 for Single/Head of Household, 2 for Married Filing Jointly) plus total qualifying children. "Effective after-tax income" equals MAGI minus net federal tax liability (or plus net refund). This tool is for illustrative purposes only and does not constitute tax advice.
