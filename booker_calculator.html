Sen. Booker's Standard Deduction Proposal

Compare net federal income tax liability under current law vs. the Booker proposal. Booker would roughly double the standard deduction and significantly expand the Child Tax Credit. The analysis assumes the standard deduction and Child Tax Credit do not phase out, since the calculator on Booker's website does not currently imply a phaseout. This analysis also does not include Booker's Earned Income Tax Credit expansion, since details are currently unavailable.
