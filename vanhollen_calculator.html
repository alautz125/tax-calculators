Sen. Van Hollen's Personal Exemption Proposal

Compare net tax liability under current law vs. the Van Hollen proposal. The proposal introduces an Alternative Maximum Tax (AMaT) — taxpayers pay the lower of regular income tax or 25.5% of income above a floor ($46k single / $64.4k HoH / $92k MFJ), with the benefit phasing out at higher incomes — plus a high-income surcharge of 5–12% on MAGI above $1M, applicable only under the Van Hollen proposal.
